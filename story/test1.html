My Europe trip in 2018

Back in 2018 when I got my master diploma I have decided to give myself a graduation gift and decided go on a Europe trip to discover the European culture. The trip has been planned by myself and it was my first solo travel which I feel proud about. In this story map my goal is to show the places I have been to, give a bit background information about each destinations and encourage/motivate other travellers to discover exciting places in Europe! The destinations were as following:

** Hungary (Budapest)

** Czech Republic (Prague)

** Germany (Berlin)

** Germany (Munich)

** Germany (Dresden)

** Netherlands (Amsterdam)

Budapest

Budapest is the capital of Hungary, one of the most beautiful cities in Europe with an amazing range of incredible things to see and do. From the historical and gorgeous building, and delicious street food to Michelin star restaurants, steam baths, and buzzing ruin bar. I guarantee you’ll fall in love with the place, boasting of its amazing attractions. It is divided by the Danube river with on one side the hilly “Buda” area and the flat “Pest” side. I have spent only 3 days and I'd say it was the best choice to start my trip from there.
